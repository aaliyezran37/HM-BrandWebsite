Here are some frequently asked question about our company.

  1. What does H&M stand for?

    2. H&M stands for Hennes & Mauritz. The company was originally founded as Hennes in 1947 and later acquired the Mauritz Widforss store in 1968, which is why the name includes both parts.

  2. Where is H&M headquartered?

    3. H&M's headquarters is located in Stockholm, Sweden.

  3. What types of products does H&M sell?

    4. H&M offers a wide range of products including clothing, accessories, shoes and home goods for men, women, teenagers and children.

  4. Does H&M have an online store?

    5. Yes, H&M has an online store that allows customers to shop for products, check availability and place orders directly through their website.

  6. Does H&M offer a loyalty program?

    7. Yes, H&M has a loyalty program called H&M Club which allows members to earn points for purchases, receive exclusive offers and get early access to sales.

  7. How does H&M ensure sustainability?

    8. H&M is committed to sustainability and has initiatives such as using organic and recycled materials, promoting circular fashion and aiming to reduce waste. They also offer a garment collecting program where customers can bring in old clothes for recycling.

  8. Can I track my order from H&M?

    9. Yes, customers can track their orders through the H&M website or app by entering their order number and email address.

  9. Does H&M offer international shipping?

    10. H&M provides international shipping to several countries. Customers should check their website for specific details regarding shipping options and fees for their location.

  10. How can I find my nearest H&M store?

    11. You can find the nearest H&M store by using the store locator feature on their official website. Simply enter your location to get a list of nearby stores.

  11. What payment methods does H&M accept?

    12. H&M accepts various payment methods including credit and debit cards, PayPal and other digital payment options depending on the country.

  12. Does H&M have a size guide?

    13. Yes, H&M provides a size guide on their website to help customers choose the right sizes for clothing, shoes and accessories.

  13. Can I cancel or modify my order?

    14. Once an order is placed, H&M typically cannot modify or cancel it. However, customers can return items according to the return policy once they receive their order.

  14. Does H&M offer any discounts for students?

    15. H&M occasionally offers student discounts but these promotions may vary by location and time. Students can check H&M's website or subscribe to their newsletter for updates on current offers.

  15. How can I contact H&M customer service?

    16. Customers can contact H&M customer service through their official website, by phone or via email. They also have a help center that provides answers to common questions.
