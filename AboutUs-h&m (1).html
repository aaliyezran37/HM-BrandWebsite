ABOUT US

H & M Hennes & Mauritz AB, commonly known as H&M, is a globally recognized multinational fashion retailer with its headquarters situated in Stockholm, Sweden. Established in 1947 by Erling Persson, the company initially operated under the name "Hennes," which is Swedish for "hers," focusing exclusively on women's clothing. Over the decades, H&M has evolved into one of the world's leading fashion brands, offering a broad range of products including clothing, accessories, footwear, and homeware for men, women, and children.
H&M is renowned for its fast fashion business model, which emphasizes quick turnaround times from design to retail, allowing the company to offer trendy and affordable fashion items that cater to a wide audience. This model has been a key driver of the brand‚Äôs success, enabling it to maintain a competitive edge in the rapidly changing fashion industry. The company operates a vast network of stores worldwide, with a significant presence in over 75 geographical markets. Currently, H&M boasts an impressive portfolio of 4,338 stores across the globe, making it the second-largest international clothing retailer, trailing only behind Inditex, the parent company of Zara.
In addition to its brick-and-mortar stores, H&M has also expanded its reach through a strong online presence, offering e-commerce services in numerous countries. The company employs more than 100,000 people worldwide, contributing to its dynamic and diverse workforce.
In recent years, H&M has focused on sustainability and innovation, striving to reduce its environmental impact and promote ethical practices within the fashion industry. The company's leadership has also seen changes, with Daniel Erver taking over as CEO in January 2024, succeeding former CEO Helena Helmersson. Under Erver's leadership, H&M continues to navigate the challenges and opportunities of the global market while remaining committed to its vision of making fashion accessible to everyone.
